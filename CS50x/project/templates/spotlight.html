Asendorf on Cargo

Kim Asendorf

Kim Asendorf is a German artist who employs conceptual strategies and generative systems to produce abstract animations, images, and sculptures. His work revolves around the pixel, which is animated and arranged in his distinctive Pixel Sorting style. Through algorithms, Asendorf creates abstract visual systems that offer various interpretations. His creations, blending simplicity and complexity, captivate viewers and induce a mesmerizing experience. Driven by curiosity, he constantly experiments and derives satisfaction from surprising outcomes. With a background in Net Art, Asendorf values accessibility and considers the internet as his preferred medium.
